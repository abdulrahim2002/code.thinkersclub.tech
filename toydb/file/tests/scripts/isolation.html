      3 # Transactions are tested more thoroughly in the MVCC tests, this just does some

      6 # Sets up a sequence of transactions that each perform a write, and checks
      7 # what they can see.
      8 #
      9 # c1: past, committed before c4 began
     10 # c2: past, commits after c4 began
     11 # c3: past, uncommitted
     12 # c4: test transaction
     13 # c5: future, committed
     14 # c6: future, uncommitted
     15 # c7: future, AS OF version 4

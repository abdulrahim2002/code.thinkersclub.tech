      6 Insertion before/after a specific node(lets call it target). in our case it is a node whose name is greater than our newly created node's
      7 name.

      9 case 1:
     10 	list is empty.
     11 
     12 Implications:
     13 	leftend and rightend points NULL.
     14 
     15 Insertion algorithm:
     16 	create a node pointer(say n) and a node. n points node. assign node the required data.
     17 	assign leftmost and rightmost to n.
     18 
     19 Now leftmost and rightmost point to new node.and list with 1 node is created.
     20 
     21 case 2:
     22 	list has one node or more nodes.
     23 
     24 Imlication:
     25 	leftmost and rightmost are pointing to a node.
     26 	leftpointer(left) and rightpointer(right) are pointing null
     27 
     28 Insertion algorithm:
     29 	find the target node. by transversing the list.
     30 		if name in new node being checked is smaller than name in current node, it is target node.
     31 	subcase 1: the target node maybe first node.(insertion at left end)
     32 	subcase 2: the target node maybe any node.(insertion at left of tsrget node)
     33 	subcase 3: target node is not found.(insertion at last(from right) that will be right end ).
     34 
     35 	subcase 1:
     36 		left of leftend(previously first node) = new node.
     37 		right of newnode = leftend(previously first ndoe).
     38 		leffend = newnode.
     39 
     40 	subcase 2:
     41 		create a temp node pointer. we insert between temp and current node. temp is left of current node
     42 		
     43 		make the following connections:
     44 			right of temp= new node
     45 			left of newnode= temp
     46 
     47 			left of currentnode = newnode
     48 			right of newnode is current
     49 
     50 	subcase 3:
     51 		right of rightend(previously last node)= newnode
     52 		left of newnode= rightend(previously last node)
     53 		rightend = newnode
     54
